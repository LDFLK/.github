Lanka Data Foundation (LDF) is a not-for-profit organization working to make public data accessible, connected, and useful.

---

## Live Projects

Explore our active initiatives designed to make government data consumable for everyone:

| Project | Description | Link |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **OpenGINXplore** | Explore Sri Lanka's data and government structures through insightful charts, </br> timelines, and interactive visuals. | [Open App](https://openginxplore.opendata.lk/) |
| **Archives** | Search and browse through Sri Lanka's government publications by dates, categories, </br> and more. | [Open App](https://archives.opendata.lk/) |

## Get Involved

Help build the future of public goods! We are a growing community dedicated to making public data more open. Whether you are a developer, advocate, or engaged citizen, you can contribute:

* **Contribute Code:** Help us build and maintain transparency tools.
* **Contribute Data:** Assist in collecting and curating datasets for the platform.
